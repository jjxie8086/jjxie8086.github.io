About Us

At SpaceTime Makerspace, we believe that innovation knows no bounds. Nestled in the heart of creativity, we provide a vibrant and inclusive environment where makers of all skill levels can come together to bring their ideas to life. Whether you're a seasoned inventor, a curious tinkerer, or a complete novice, there's a place for you here.

Our mission is simple: to empower individuals to unleash their creativity and turn their ideas into reality. We strive to foster a community of makers who share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and inspire one another to push the boundaries of innovation.

We offer a variety of tools and accessories at SpaceTime Makerspace, you'll find a wide range of tools, equipment, and resources to support your projects. From 3D printers and laser cutters to soldering stations and woodworking tools, we have everything you need to prototype and build your creations. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to provide guidance and assistance, whether you're learning a new skill or troubleshooting a problem.

One of the things that sets SpaceTime Makerspace apart is our vibrant and welcoming community. Whether you're attending one of our workshops, participating in a hackathon, or simply hanging out in our shared workspace, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for making.

Education is at the core of what we do at SpaceTime Makerspace. We offer a variety of classes and workshops covering topics such as electronics, programming, woodworking, and more. Our goal is to provide accessible learning opportunities for people of all ages and backgrounds, helping them develop the skills they need to bring their ideas to life.

Ready to join the SpaceTime Makerspace community? Whether you're a seasoned maker or just starting out, we'd love to have you! Drop by our space to take a tour, attend an event, or sign up for a membership. With flexible membership options and a supportive community to cheer you on, there's never been a better time to start making at PWN Go Makerspace.

Stay up-to-date with the latest news, events, and projects from SpaceTime Makerspace by following us on social media and subscribing to our newsletter. We can't wait to see what you'll create!
