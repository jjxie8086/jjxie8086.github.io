Throughout this course, you'll delve into the core principles of 3D printing, from understanding the technology behind it to mastering the intricacies of designing and producing three-dimensional objects. Dive into popular software tools such as Tinkercad, Fusion 360, or Blender, and gain hands-on experience with different 3D printing methods, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Stereolithography (SLA), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), and more.
